Wenqi Fan - Openings (Year-round Recruitment)

Dr. Wenqi Fan is currently an Assistant Professor of the Department of Computing (Faculty of Engineering) & Department of Management and Marketing (Business School), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).

Our research group are actively recruiting self-motivated Postdoc, Ph.D. students, Dual PhD Degree students, MPhil/Msc, and Research Assistants, etc. Visiting scholars, interns, and self-funded students are also welcome. Send me an email if you are interested.

>>> Research areas: machine learning (ML), data mining (DM), artificial intelligence (AI), deep learning (DNNs), graph neural networks (GNNs), computer vision (CV), natural language processing (NLP), Bioinformatics, etc.

Please visit the Fellowship & Scholarship Schemes (Graduate School) website for further details. [Link]

[Admission Requirements]

  • Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme(HKPFS)，monthly stipend: HK$27,600

  • PolyU Presidential PhD Fellowship Scheme (PPPFS)，monthly stipend: HK$27,290

  • PolyU Research Postgraduate Scholarship (PRPgS), monthly stipend: HK$18,100

  • Dual PhD Degree Programmes, monthly stipend: HK$18,470

  • Several Research Assistant (RA) positions are available now!

Welcome to drop me an email with your CV!

Application Process:

If you are interested in a position, please feel free to contact Wenqi at: wenqi.fan(at)polyu(dot)edu(dot)hk.

Below are some suggestions when contacting via email:

Please…

  1. Use a subject line as follows: {$position} Open Position {$YourName}， where {$position} should be ‘‘Postdoc’’, ‘‘PhD’’, ‘‘MPhil’’, ‘‘MSs’’, ‘‘BS’’, ‘‘Visiting’’, or ‘‘RA’’.
    e.g., If I were applying for a Research Assistant position I would use the subject ‘‘RA Open Position Wenqi Fan’’

  2. Provide a few sentences to introduce yourself and express your interest in the position

  3. Attach your CV/Resume Including the following (if applicable): GPA/ranking, TOEFL/IELTS/GRE scores, and publications/research experience, awards, etc.

Thank you and I look forward to hearing from you!

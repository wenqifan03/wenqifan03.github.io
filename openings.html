Wenqi Fan - Openings

I am a Research Assistant Professor of the Department of Computing (COMP), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).

Our research group are actively recruiting self-motivated Postdoc, Ph.D. students, Joint Ph.D. students (i.e., UTS、CAS、SUSTech, etc.), MPhil/Msc, and Research Assistants. Visiting scholars and interns are also welcome. Send me an email if you are interested. Please visit the Fellowship & Scholarship Schemes website for further details.

  • Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme(HKPFS)，monthly stipend: HK$26,600

  • Joint PhD Programme, monthly stipend: HK$18,100

  • PolyU Research Postgraduate Scholarship (PRPgS), monthly stipend: HK$18,100

  • Several Research Assistant positions are available now!

Welcome to drop me an email with your CV!

Application Process:

If you are interested in a position, please feel free to contact Wenqi at: wenqifan(at)polyu(dot)edu(dot)hk.

Below are some suggestions when contacting via email:

Please…

  1. Use a subject line as follows: {$position} Open Position {$YourName}， where {$position} should be ‘‘Postdoc’’, ‘‘PhD’’, ‘‘MPhil’’, ‘‘MSs’’, ‘‘BS’’, ‘‘Visiting’’, or ‘‘RA’’ e.g., If I were applying for a Research Assistant position I would use the subject ‘‘RA Open Position Wenqi Fan’’

  2. Provide a few sentences to introduce yourself and express your interest in the position

  3. Attach your CV/Resume Including the following (if applicable): GPA/ranking, GRE/TOEFL/IELTS scores, and publications/research experience

Thank you and I look forward to hearing from you!

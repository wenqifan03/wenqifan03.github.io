Wenqi Fan

 

Dr. Wenqi Fan is currently a Research Assistant Professor (RAP) of the Department of Computing (COMP), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). Before joining the Department, he worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow at PolyU. He received his Ph.D. degree in computer science from the City University of Hong Kong (CityU) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Qing LI and Prof. Jianping WANG. From 2018 to 2021, he was a research scholar of Data Science and Engineering (DSE) Lab at Michigan State University (MSU), under the supervision of Prof. Jiliang Tang.
